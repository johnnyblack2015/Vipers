CLUB RULES

Constitution & Code of Conduct

I. Membership & Eligibility (VV-MEM)

  • VV-MEM-01 Age Criteria: Membership is open to individuals aged 16 years and above. The maximum age for active playing membership is 50 years. Any player aged 50 must provide a valid medical fitness certificate.
  • VV-MEM-02 Documentation: All applicants must provide valid government-issued ID proof (Aadhaar/Passport/Voter ID) at registration.
  • VV-MEM-03 Non-Transferability: Membership is individual and cannot be transferred or shared.

II. Code of Conduct (VV-CON)

  • VV-CON-01Spirit of Cricket: Players must maintain the highest standard of sportsmanship.
  • VV-CON-02Umpire Interaction: The Umpire’s decision is final. Only the Captain may discuss a decision.
  • VV-CON-03Substance Policy: Alcohol, tobacco, or banned substances are strictly forbidden during matches or practice.
  • VV-CON-04Equipment Misconduct: Intentional damage to kit will result in a fine equivalent to one match fee.
  • VV-CON-05Social Media: Members shall not post content that brings the Volt Vipers into disrepute.

III. Payments & Financials (VV-PAY)

  • VV-PAY-01Registration: ₹2,000/- INR (One-time). Covers official jersey and kit access.
  • VV-PAY-02Yearly Subscription: ₹1,000/- INR due by Jan 10th annually.
  • VV-PAY-03Late Payment: Grace period until Jan 15th. Failure results in suspension.
  • VV-PAY-04Match Day Opt-in: Match fees must be sent immediately upon polling to confirm spot.

IV. Refund & Cancellation (VV-REF)

VV-REF-01 (Opt-out Policy):

  • >72 Hours: Full refund.
  • 24-72 Hours: Refund only if a replacement is found.
  • <24 Hours / No-Show: Non-refundable.

V. Match Day (VV-MAT)

  • VV-MAT-01Arrive 20 mins prior to start.
  • VV-MAT-02Only official Volt Vipers jersey allowed.

VII. Equipment (VV-EQP)

  • VV-EQP-01Helmets mandatory for batsmen & keepers standing up.
  • VV-EQP-03Intentional damage to kit must be paid for in full.

VI. Disciplinary Actions (VV-DIS)

Code Offense Penalty
VV-DIS-01 Minor Dissent / Lateness Batsmen: -2 places | Bowlers: -1 over
VV-DIS-02 Medium Breach (Arguing w/ Umpire) 1-match suspension
VV-DIS-03 Serious Breach (Substance/Physical) Fine (1 match fee) + 3-match suspension
VV-DIS-04 Severe Breach (Fraud/Violence) Permanent Expulsion

FAQ

How do I join the Volt Vipers?

Joining is easy. Click the 'Register' button at the top of the page and fill out the form to get started. Once submitted, you'll undergo a phone screening with our team. Successful applicants will then be invited to trials in the nets, where our coaches will assess your skills to determine your selection for the squad.

What are the fees?

Joining is straightforward. To get started, there is a trial nets fee of 300/-. If you are selected for the squad following your trials, the annual membership fee is 2000/-. This fee ensures you are fully registered and ready for the season.

Where do you play matches?

We currently compete in tournaments staged across key venues in Aziz Nagar, Moinabad, and Chilkur.

Do I get to switch batting to bowling in matches?

If you are an all-rounder, please note that we will prioritize the specific skill for which you were selected during trials. While we may consider requests to switch roles based on team vacancy and player availability, you should expect to fill only one primary role—either Batter or Bowler—per match.

What equipment do I need to bring?

While membership includes access to the shared team kit, we highly encourage players to invest in their own equipment for personal comfort and performance.

If you do not have a personal kit, you must—for hygiene and safety reasons—provide your own:

  • Batsman’s inner gloves
  • Head bandana
  • Abdomen guard
